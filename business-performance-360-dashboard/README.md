
📊 BUSINESS PERFORMANCE 360° – End-to-End BI & Analytics Project

📌 Project Overview
BUSINESS PERFORMANCE 360° is a complete end-to-end Business Intelligence and Data Analytics project built using Python, SQL Server, Power BI, DAX, and Excel.
The objective of this project is to simulate a real-world business reporting environment where decision-makers can monitor:
•	Revenue Performance
•	Profitability
•	Geographic Performance
•	Operational Risk
•	Product Analytics
•	Seasonal Business Impact
This project was designed using the STAR Method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to improve business storytelling and executive-level dashboard reporting.

🎯 STAR Method Application
Situation
Business leaders lacked a centralized system to monitor sales, operational risks, profitability, and regional performance.
Task
Develop a professional BI solution capable of tracking business performance and supporting executive-level decision-making.
Action
•	Generated synthetic retail business data using Python
•	Performed SQL-based ETL and KPI engineering
•	Built interactive Power BI dashboards
•	Implemented DAX measures and business logic
•	Applied storytelling-driven dashboard design principles
Result
Created a complete executive-level analytics solution capable of:
•	Monitoring business growth
•	Identifying operational risks
•	Tracking product profitability
•	Supporting strategic decisions
•	Delivering actionable business insights
